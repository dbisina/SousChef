The Problem: Recipe Chaos in the Digital Age

In today's digital landscape, recipe content has exploded across social media platforms. TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts have transformed cooking content from text-based blogs into engaging, short-form videos that garner billions of views. However, this shift has created a significant pain point for home cooks: recipe videos are entertaining but functionally inadequate for actual cooking.

The Core Problems

üì±
Fragmented Storage

Manual Interaction
Traditional recipe apps require constant screen touching with messy hands while cooking, leading to frustration and damaged devices.
üìù
Shopping List Burden

Market Validation

According to recent industry research, 72% of millennials and Gen Z users discover recipes through social media videos, yet only 38% successfully cook from these videos without supplementary written instructions. This represents a massive opportunity to bridge the gap between discovery and execution.
