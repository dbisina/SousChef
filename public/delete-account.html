Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your SousChef account and all associated data at any time.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile information and preferences
  • Saved recipes and cookbooks
  • Meal plans and shopping lists
  • Pantry items and waste tracking data
  • Cooking streaks and gamification progress
  • Comments and social interactions

How to delete in the app

1 Open SousChef and go to the More tab
2 Scroll down and tap Delete Account
3 Confirm deletion — your account and data will be permanently removed

Can't access the app? Request deletion by email:

✉️ Request via Email

Deletion requests are processed within 48 hours. This action is irreversible — all data will be permanently erased.
