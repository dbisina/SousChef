## Introduction

Daniel Bisina ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by SousChef.

This Privacy Policy applies to our website, and its associated subdomains (collectively, our "Service") alongside our application, SousChef. By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service.

## Information We Collect and Use

To provide you with the best experience, we may collect the following types of information:

### 1. Personal Information
When you register for an account, we may ask for personal information such as:
- **Name / Username:** To identify you within the app.
- **Email Address:** To manage your account, authenticate you via Firebase, and send important updates.

### 2. User Content
- **Photos and Videos:** We access your camera and photo library only when you explicitly choose to scan a recipe or ingredient. These images are processed to extract culinary information.
- **Audio:** We access your microphone only when you activate voice commands. Audio data is processed to execute your cooking commands.

### 3. Usage and Analytics Data
- **Usage Data:** We collect information about how you interact with our Service, such as the features you use and the time spent on the app. This helps us improve the user experience.
- **Device Information:** We may collect information about the device you use to access the Service, including the hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers (for push notifications and subscription management).
- **Purchase History:** If you make a purchase (such as a subscription), we process this data through RevenueCat and the App Store. We do not store your credit card information directly.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our Service.
- Process your recipe imports and ingredient scans.
- Manage your account and subscriptions.
- Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
- Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Service.

## Third-Party Services
We may employ third-party companies and individuals due to the following reasons:
- **Firebase (Google):** For authentication, database storage, and analytics.

These third parties have access to your Personal Information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

## Children's Privacy

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we will be able to do necessary actions.
